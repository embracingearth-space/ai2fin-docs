# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with AI2Fin in minutes. This guide will walk you through the essential steps to start managing your finances intelligently.

## Step 1: Access the Platform

1. **Visit the Application**: Go to [app.ai2fin.com](https://app.ai2fin.com)
2. **Sign Up**: Create your account with email and password
3. **Verify Email**: Check your inbox and verify your email address
4. **Login**: Access your AI2Fin dashboard

## Step 2: Set Up Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure User Preferences">
    Click on your profile avatar in the top-right corner and select "Preferences"
    
    **Essential Settings:**
    - **Country**: Select your country for currency and tax calculations
    - **Currency**: Choose your primary currency
    - **Date Format**: Set your preferred date format
    - **GST Settings**: Configure GST rates if applicable
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore the Dashboard">
    The main dashboard provides an overview of your financial data:
    
    - **Transaction Statistics**: Total transactions, amounts, and categories
    - **Recent Activity**: Latest transactions and bills
    - **Quick Actions**: Create expenses, bills, and trips
    - **System Status**: AI services and connectivity status
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Configure Categories

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Default Categories">
    AI2Fin comes with pre-configured categories:
    - **Business Expenses**: Office supplies, software, utilities
    - **Travel**: Transport, accommodation, meals
    - **Professional**: Training, certifications, tools
    - **Tax-Deductible**: Items eligible for tax deductions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Custom Categories">
    1. Navigate to **Categories** in the sidebar
    2. Click **"Add Category"**
    3. Enter category name and description
    4. Set color and icon for easy identification
    5. Configure tax deductibility settings
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 7: Set Up Recurring Bills

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify Recurring Transactions">
    Look for transactions that repeat monthly, quarterly, or annually
    - Rent/mortgage payments
    - Utility bills
    - Software subscriptions
    - Insurance payments
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Bill Patterns">
    1. Go to **Patterns (Recurring)** in the sidebar
    2. Click **"Create Bill Pattern"**
    3. Enter bill details:
       - Name and description
       - Expected amount
       - Frequency (monthly, quarterly, etc.)
       - Due date
    4. Link to existing transactions
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 8: Configure Tax Settings

<Warning>
Tax settings are crucial for accurate financial reporting and compliance.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable GST Management">
    1. Go to **User Preferences**
    2. Navigate to **Tax Settings**
    3. Enable **GST Management**
    4. Set your GST rate (e.g., 10% for Australia)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Tax Categories">
    Mark categories as tax-deductible:
    - Business expenses
    - Professional development
    - Equipment purchases
    - Travel expenses
  </Step>
</Steps>
