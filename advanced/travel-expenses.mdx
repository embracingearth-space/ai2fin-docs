## ATO Compliance

### Logbook Requirements

<Warning>
ATO requires detailed logbooks for certain types of travel claims.
</Warning>

**When Logbook is Required:**
- **Cents per km method**: No logbook required
- **Actual cost method**: Logbook required
- **12-week logbook**: For car expenses
- **5-year logbook**: For long-term claims

### Required Documentation

<Steps>

## Tax Optimization

### Deduction Strategies

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Cents per km Method">
    **Benefits:**
    - Simple calculation
    - No logbook required
    - Fixed rate per km
    - Easy to claim
    
    **Limitations:**
    - Maximum 5,000 km per year
    - Fixed rate may not cover actual costs
    - Limited to cars and motorcycles
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Actual Cost Method">
    **Benefits:**
    - Claim actual costs
    - Higher deductions possible
    - Include all vehicle costs
    - More accurate for high usage
    
    **Requirements:**
    - Detailed logbook
    - Receipts for all costs
    - Business use percentage
    - 12-week logbook period
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
